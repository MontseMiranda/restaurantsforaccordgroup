Julivert Meu

Julivert Meu is an old Catalan farmhouse transported to the Barcelona's city center. A restaurant that since 1970 serves excellent traditional and homemade food. Julivert Meu represents the best of Catalan gastronomy and the spirit of good eating..

The menu of Julivert Meu includes classic starters, such as xatonada, Iberian cold cuts or escalivada, and a fresh assortment of salads and carpaccios. And as in any good farm, could not miss a good ember to roast excellent meats of all kinds: beef, lamb, chicken, pork, etc. In short, a quality offer at affordable prices..

Julivert Meu is housed in a three-storey building with rustic and homely decor, just one block from Las Ramblas in Barcelona, ​​a very central location.

Location: 46km from Alicante Airport

Dining room capacities: 25,30,50 pax

Price Level: average-high

Style: Mix of Mediterranean and author cuisine
